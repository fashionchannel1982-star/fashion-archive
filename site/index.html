The idea

Fashion's memory is locked in time. The shows happened. The footage exists. But almost none of it is findable, connectable, or properly credited.

Fashion Archive lets you search decades of runway in plain language — "sheer black tailoring", "structured shoulders" — and find the exact moving moment. Follow a house's signature codes as they shift across the years. And see every moment carried with its source, its season and its maker.

It's built for the people who make, study and steward fashion: designers and houses, students and schools, researchers, buyers and the press. Not another image library — a living, intelligent, credited record of how fashion actually evolved.
